Greek Salad Recipe

Description

A fresh and colorful Mediterranean classic, Greek salad combines crisp cucumber, juicy tomatoes, red onion, briny olives, and creamy feta cheese, all brought together with a drizzle of olive oil and a sprinkle of oregano. Light, refreshing, and full of vibrant flavor, it’s perfect as a side dish or a healthy main on warm days.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes (3–4 medium, chopped or cut into wedges)
  • Cucumber (1 large, sliced into half-moons)
  • Red onion (½, thinly sliced)
  • Green bell pepper (1, sliced, optional but authentic)
  • Kalamata olives (½ cup)
  • Feta cheese (150–200 g, cubed or crumbled)
  • Extra virgin olive oil (3–4 tbsp)
  • Dried oregano (1 tsp)
  • Salt & black pepper (to taste)
  • Red wine vinegar (1 tbsp, optional)

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the tomatoes into wedges or bite-sized pieces.
  2. Slice the cucumber into half-moons and thinly slice the red onion.
  3. Cut the green bell pepper into thin strips (if using).
  4. Combine tomatoes, cucumber, onion, bell pepper, and olives in a large salad bowl.
  5. Add the feta cheese on top, either in cubes or crumbled.
  6. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and red wine vinegar (if using).
  7. Sprinkle with dried oregano, season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Toss gently (or serve as is with feta on top) and enjoy fresh.
