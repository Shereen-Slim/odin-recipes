Pizza Recipe

Description

An Italian classic loved worldwide, pizza is made with a crisp yet chewy dough base topped with rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and endless topping possibilities — from fresh vegetables and herbs to savory meats. Baked until golden and bubbling, pizza is versatile, comforting, and perfect for sharing.

Ingredients

Dough (1 large or 2 small)
  • All-purpose flour (2½ cups / 315 g), plus extra for dusting
  • Warm water ~40°C (1 cup / 240 ml)
  • Instant yeast (2¼ tsp / 7 g)
  • Sugar or honey (1 tsp)
  • Fine salt (1 tsp)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp)
Quick tomato sauce
  • Crushed tomatoes or passata (1 cup / 240 ml)
  • Garlic, minced (1 clove)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp)
  • Salt & pepper (to taste)
  • Dried oregano or basil (1 tsp)
Toppings
  • Fresh mozzarella, sliced or shredded (200 g)
  • Fresh basil leaves (a handful)
  • Parmesan, grated (2 tbsp, optional)
  • Olive oil (for drizzling)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add yeast mixture and olive oil, then mix into a dough.
  3. Knead dough on a floured surface for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1–2 hours until doubled in size.
  5. Meanwhile, prepare the sauce: sauté garlic in olive oil, add crushed tomatoes, season with herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  6. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Place a baking tray or pizza stone inside to heat.
  7. Punch down dough and roll it into a thin circle or rectangle on parchment paper.
  8. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the base, leaving edges uncovered.
  9. Add mozzarella slices and any extra toppings of choice.
  10. Carefully transfer pizza onto the hot tray/stone and bake for 12–15 minutes, until crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  11. Remove from oven, top with fresh basil, a drizzle of olive oil, and serve hot.
