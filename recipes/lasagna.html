Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian comfort dish made with layers of tender pasta sheets, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and savory meat or vegetables, all baked together under a golden, melted cheese topping. Hearty, flavorful, and satisfying, it’s a crowd-pleasing favorite for family meals or gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets (12-15)
  • Ground beef (500g / 1 lb) or beef & pork mix
  • Olive oil (2 tbsp)
  • Onion (1, finely chopped)
  • Garlic (2–3 cloves, minced)
  • Carrot (1 small, finely diced, optional)
  • Celery (1 stalk, finely diced, optional)
  • Tomato paste (2 tbsp)
  • Crushed tomatoes or passata (800g / 2 cans)
  • Dried oregano (1 tsp)
  • Dried basil (1 tsp)
  • Dried thyme (1 tsp)
  • Salt & black pepper (to taste)
  • Sugar (1/2 tsp, optional)
Bechamel (white sauce)
  • Butter (4 tbsp)
  • Flour (4 tbsp)
  • Milk, warmed (4 cups / 1 liter)
  • Nutmeg (pinch, optional)
  • Salt & pepper (to taste)
Cheese Layer
  • Mozzarella, shredded (2 cups)
  • Parmesan, grated (1 cup)
  • Ricotta (1½ cups, optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan. Add chopped onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Sauté until softened.
  3. Add ground beef (or beef & pork mix) and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato paste, then add crushed tomatoes, herbs, salt, pepper, and a pinch of sugar. Simmer for 20–30 minutes until sauce thickens.
  5. Meanwhile, prepare the béchamel: melt butter in a saucepan, whisk in flour, and cook for 1–2 minutes. Gradually add warm milk, whisking until smooth. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg. Simmer until slightly thickened.
  6. Lightly grease a baking dish. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
  7. Layer lasagna sheets over the sauce, followed by more meat sauce, a layer of béchamel, and a sprinkle of mozzarella/ricotta. Repeat layers until ingredients are used, finishing with béchamel on top.
  8. Sprinkle generously with mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 20–25 minutes, until golden and bubbling.
  10. Let rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing and serving.
